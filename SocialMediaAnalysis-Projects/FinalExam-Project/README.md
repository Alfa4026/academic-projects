# **Preprocessing & Dataset Overview**

## **Background**

Advan is a well-known local Indonesian smartphone brand that once dominated the market. However, the rapid rise of global competitors and misinformation regarding its supposed bankruptcy significantly impacted its reputation. Through major innovation and strategic rebranding efforts, Advan has been attempting to rise again with a refreshed identity and new technological breakthroughs.

---

## **Why This Analysis Matters**

This analysis is important for several reasons:

* To measure public perception **before and after** Advan‚Äôs revival.
* To identify **key discussion topics** such as specifications, design, performance, pricing, and camera quality.
* To evaluate **shifts in sentiment trends** across different time periods.
* To provide **data-driven insights** that support branding and marketing strategies.

---

## **Dataset**

To understand public sentiment surrounding Advan‚Äôs brand performance before and after its revival in 2020, this project uses YouTube comments as the primary data source. The data was collected using the **YouTube Data API**, enabling structured and standardized extraction of public comments.
